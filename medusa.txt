MedusaJs




Medusa is an open-source commerce platform with a built-in framework for customization

Medusa's building blocks 
    1) Commerce modules : cart, product, order modules,....
    2) A core package : Responsible for orchestrating the different commerce modules, extending data models, and exposing REST API's

What can be build with Medusa
    1) Ecommerce
    2) Marketplace
    3) Subscription business
    4) Order management service
    5) B2B store
    6) Point of Sales (POS)



npx create-medusa-app@latest

npx medusa db:generate blog

npx medusa db:migrate
